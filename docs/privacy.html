Neural_OS Privacy Policy

ScriptMasterLabs  |  Effective Date: June 1, 2026  |  Last Updated: June 1, 2026

Neural_OS ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is an institutional-grade RWA settlement and AI trading intelligence platform developed by ScriptMasterLabs. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Neural_OS:

  • Wallet Addresses: Public blockchain wallet addresses you connect via WalletConnect. We never access private keys or seed phrases.
  • Transaction Data: On-chain transaction hashes, amounts, and timestamps associated with your connected wallet. This data is read directly from public blockchains (Ethereum, Base, XRP Ledger, zkSync, HyperEVM, ZetaChain).
  • Subscription and Billing Information: Subscription tier selections and USDC payment records on-chain. Payments are processed entirely on-chain; we do not store credit card numbers or traditional payment credentials.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized logs of feature interactions (e.g., which AI agents were activated, pages visited) to improve app performance.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility purposes.
  • API Keys (BYOK): If you opt in to Bring Your Own Key (available to Sovereign and Institutional tier subscribers), API keys you provide are stored encrypted on your device and are never transmitted to our servers in plaintext.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Neural_OS platform and its AI agent features.
  • To calculate subscription tiers, loyalty program status, and applicable protocol fee discounts.
  • To display your wallet balances, transaction history, and market intelligence data.
  • To route protocol fees (0.1%–1% of transaction volume, depending on tier) to the designated billing address.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Blockchain Data

Neural_OS interacts with public blockchains. Blockchain transactions are inherently public and permanent. Any wallet address you connect and any transactions you execute are visible on-chain to all participants. We have no ability to modify or delete blockchain records.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Alchemy: Blockchain RPC provider used to read on-chain data. Subject to Alchemy's Privacy Policy.
  • WalletConnect: Wallet connection infrastructure. Subject to WalletConnect's Privacy Policy.
  • CoinGecko: Market price data (ETH, BTC, XRP, $AIXBT). Requests are read-only and do not include wallet identifiers.
  • Stripe: Used for optional fiat subscription billing where applicable. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregated usage statistics that cannot identify you individually. We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users or the public.

6. Data Retention

Usage logs are retained for up to 90 days and then automatically deleted. Blockchain transaction data is not stored by us — it is read in real time from public blockchains. If you disconnect your wallet, we cease processing your wallet data going forward.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted transmission (TLS), on-device encrypted storage for sensitive credentials, and zero custody of user private keys. We never request or store seed phrases, private keys, or signing credentials.

8. Children's Privacy

Neural_OS is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to blockchain immutability limitations).
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above and, where required, by providing in-app notification. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
