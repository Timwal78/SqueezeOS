How It Works
  • Cast @tipmaster register <your-xrpl-address> on Farcaster to link your wallet.
  • Pay the one-time 15 RLUSD setup fee to unlock P2P tipping.
  • Tip any registered creator — TipMaster generates a Xaman payment link. You sign, funds go directly to their wallet. No middleman ever touches the RLUSD.
  • Every tip is recorded for leaderboard and stats. Weekly top-10 automatically broadcast to Farcaster every Friday.
  • Developers: resolve any Farcaster username to an XRPL wallet via the public API.
Developer API
GET
/api/resolve/{username}
Resolve a Farcaster username to their registered XRPL wallet address. Returns wallet_address and chain. Use this to route autonomous agent tips directly to creators without asking for wallet addresses.
GET
/api/leaderboard?period=week|alltime&limit=10
Top tippers by RLUSD volume. Supports weekly and all-time views. Max 25 results.
GET
/api/user/{fid}
Look up a Farcaster user by FID — returns their wallet address and account activation status.
GET
/api/status
Service health, version, uptime, and feature flags.
