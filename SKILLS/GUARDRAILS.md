# Skills / Guardrails

## Product guardrail
Never reduce ARGUS OMEGA into a retail indicator bundle, a simple long/short API, or a dashboard-first toy.

## Math guardrail
Use explicit formulas and bounded normalized scores.
Every score must be clamped to valid ranges.

## Deception guardrail
High deception must suppress conviction and action aggression.
Do not allow bullish alignment to override extreme deception.

## Liquidity guardrail
Liquidity Ghost should influence execution posture and trigger placement, not just directional bias.

## Historical analog guardrail
ECHO strength depends on similarity AND confidence. Historical matches alone are not enough.

## Narrative guardrail
Narratives must read like a machine intelligence briefing:
- concise
- analytical
- no slang
- no emojis
- no retail hype

## Action-class guardrail
Action classes describe posture and timing, not simplistic entry commands.

## Testing guardrail
Every formula family needs tests:
- clamping
- penalties
- scenario ordering
- action mapping
- contradiction handling
